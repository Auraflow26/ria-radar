Positioning

Why they're alts-ready

  • Already in the alts business: the firm advises 2 private funds with $12M in gross assets, confirming existing infrastructure and GP/fund familiarity.
  • 97% discretionary control over $6.8B in AUM means advisors can move capital without client-by-client approval — a critical enabler for illiquid, subscription-based vehicles.
  • 89% of the firm's $7.0B RAUM is HNW individual assets ($6.2B), the precise client segment with both the accreditation status and risk tolerance for alternative allocations.
  • Custody held at both Charles Schwab and Fidelity — both operate dedicated alternative investment platforms for advisors, providing existing operational rails for alts onboarding.

Suggested angle
