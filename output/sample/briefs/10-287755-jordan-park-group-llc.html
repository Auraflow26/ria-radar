Positioning

Jordan Park Group LLC is a San Francisco-based ultra-high-net-worth RIA managing $23.3B in total regulatory AUM, nearly all of it ($23.2B) on a fully discretionary basis. The firm serves a concentrated book of 163 HNW individuals, implying a very high average relationship size, and operates as a sophisticated multi-family-office-style platform that already advises 45 private funds with $6.6B in gross assets.

Why they're alts-ready

  • Deep private-fund infrastructure already in place: Jordan Park advises 45 private funds — 29 private equity, 5 hedge, and 6 real estate — with $6.6B in gross assets reported on Form ADV.
  • Virtually the entire book is managed on a discretionary basis: $23.2B of $23.3B total RAUM is discretionary, giving the firm direct authority to allocate to new strategies.
  • HNW client concentration is extreme: $15.5B — 67% of total RAUM — is attributed to HNW individuals, the exact client segment that evergreen alternative vehicles are designed to serve.
  • Custodial infrastructure supports alts delivery at scale: the firm custodies at Charles Schwab, which operates the Schwab Alternative Investment Marketplace, and at Fidelity, which operates an alternative investments platform for advisors.

Current alternatives footprint

Per Form ADV Item 7.B, Jordan Park Group advises private funds and self-reports 45 funds under management: 29 private equity funds, 5 hedge funds, and 6 real estate funds, with combined gross assets of $6.6B. This represents a substantial, institutionally-structured alternatives program — not a token allocation. The firm custodies at both Charles Schwab (Schwab Alternative Investment Marketplace) and Fidelity (alternative investments platform for advisors), indicating established operational pathways for delivering alternative products to clients.

Suggested angle

With $23.2B in discretionary AUM spread across just 163 HNW clients and an existing 45-fund private portfolio generating $6.6B in gross assets, Jordan Park is already operating at the highest end of alternatives sophistication — the conversation should be about complementing or expanding their existing program, not introducing the concept. An evergreen vehicle could appeal as a liquidity-managed, operationally simpler wrapper alongside their bespoke fund structures, particularly given their dual custodial presence on platforms that already support alternative investment delivery.
